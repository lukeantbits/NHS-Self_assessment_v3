Hep C self-assessment

Could you have hepatitis C?

Hepatitis C is a virus that can infect and damage the liver.


In most cases, it causes no noticeable symptoms until the liver has been significantly damaged. When symptoms do occur, they are often vague and can be easily mistaken for another condition. 


If you suspect you may have hepatitis C, complete this assessment to learn more about the signs you should be looking out for.
