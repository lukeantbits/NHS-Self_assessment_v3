Blood pressure quiz

Test your knowledge

Around 30% of people in England have high blood pressure but many don't know it. If left untreated, high blood pressure increases your risk of a heart attack or stroke.

It's important to know what affects our blood pressure and what you can do to keep your blood pressure at a healthy level.

Take this short quiz and see how much you know. You can check your answers as you go and you'll get a score at the end.
