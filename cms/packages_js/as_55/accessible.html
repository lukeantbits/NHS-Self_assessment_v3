Healthy workplace assessment

How healthy is your workplace?

Health and safety is vital in a workplace and can protect and even improve the health of its employees.


Answer the following questions to assess your workplace. Questions 1-9 will assess whether your workplace is following the UK’s health and safety laws. 

Questions 10-12 cover issues to do 

with overall health and wellbeing.
           


At the end you’ll get a list of things 

to raise with your employer.

Questions

  1. Let's start with your work area. Is there sufficient space to work comfortably?
    1. Yes, I have plenty of room (Add 5 points)
    2. No, my work area is tiny and cramped
      • Your work area could be improved
    3. It's OK but could be improved (Add 2 points)
      • Your work area could be improved
  2. A workplace shouldn’t be cooler than 16C, how would you rate the temperature and ventilation in your workplace?
    1. Just right (Add 5 points)
    2. Too hot and stuffy
      • The temperature needs addressing
    3. Changeable and unpredictable (Add 2 points)
      • The temperature needs addressing
    4. Cold and draughty
      • The temperature needs addressing
  3. How would you rate the lighting in the workplace?
    1. Just right (Add 5 points)
    2. Overly bright
      • The lighting needs adjustment
    3. Too dark
      • The lighting needs adjustment
  4. Which of the following can you control? You can pick one or more
    1. Heating (Add 2 points)
      • Request a health and safety assessment
    2. Lighting (Add 2 points)
      • Request a health and safety assessment
    3. Ventilation (Add 2 points)
      • Request a health and safety assessment
    4. Chair height (Add 2 points)
      • Request a health and safety assessment
    5. None of these
  5. How often are working areas cleaned and waste removed?
    1. Daily (Add 5 points)
    2. Weekly (Add 2 points)
    3. Monthly
      • Cleaning and waste collection could be better
    4. Never
      • Cleaning and waste collection could be better
  6. Is there is a supply of easily accessible drinking water or water coolers?
    1. Yes (Add 5 points)
    2. No
      • Drinking water needs to be provided
    3. Don't know
      • Drinking water needs to be provided
  7. Are the toilets clean and in good working order?
    1. Very clean and checked regularly (Add 5 points)
    2. Moderately clean but could be checked more often (Add 2 points)
      • The toilets could be cleaned more regularly
    3. Not very clean and rarely checked
      • The toilets could be cleaned more regularly
    4. Really dirty and are never checked or cleaned
      • The toilets could be cleaned more regularly
  8. Does your workplace have a ‘rest’ or seating area for use during breaks?
    1. Yes, we have a seated area and a private room if we need to lie down (Add 5 points)
    2. Yes, we use the canteen for breaks (Add 5 points)
    3. No, there is no separate area for us to use during our breaks
      • A rest area for breaks is needed
  9. If there’s an accident at work are there adequate first aid facilities?
    1. There’s a first aid kit and trained first aider (Add 5 points)
    2. There's a first aid kit but not sure about a trained first aider (Add 2 points)
      • There is a need for a first aid kit and a first aider
    3. I'm not sure if we have a first aid kit
      • There is a need for a first aid kit and a first aider
    4. There is no first aid kit or first aider
      • There is a need for a first aid kit and a first aider
  10. Does your employer encourage healthier eating?
    1. Yes, there's a canteen with a good choice and signs to show what's most healthy (Add 5 points)
    2. There's a canteen but the food isn't very healthy and choice is limited
      • The canteen food could be healthier
    3. There's no canteen but my employer does try to encourage healthier eating (Add 3 points)
    4. There's no canteen and my employer doesn't encourage healthier eating
      • More could be done to encourage healthier eating
  11. Which of the following has your employer done to encourage physical activity? (Pick one or more)
    1. Provided gym access or subsidised membership (Add 5 points)
    2. Promoted active travel to work (Add 5 points)
    3. Set up exercise classes or sports teams (Add 5 points)
    4. Any initiative similar to those listed above (Add 5 points)
    5. Put up posters encouraging stair use (Add 2 points)
    6. None of these things
      • More could be done to encourage physical activity
  12. Which of the following does your employer offer? (Pick one or more)
    1. Arranges health-related events (Add 2 points)
    2. Arranges individual health checks (Add 2 points)
    3. An Occupational Health Service, access to an Employee Advisory Service, or similar (Add 5 points)
    4. Promotes NHS health checks and screening programmes (Add 2 points)
    5. Promotes mental wellbeing at work (Add 2 points)
    6. Provides access to counselling (Add 2 points)
    7. Smoking cessation services (Add 2 points)
    8. None of these
      • More could be done to promote general health and wellbeing
