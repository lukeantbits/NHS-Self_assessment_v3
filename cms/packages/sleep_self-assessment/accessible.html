Sleep self-assessment

Do you have a sleep problem?


Questions

  1. Thinking about a typical night in the last month, how long does it take you to fall asleep?

    2. 16-30 minutes (Add 3 points)
    3. 31-45 minutes (Add 2 points)
    4. 46-60 minutes (Add 1 points)
    5. More than 61 minutes
  2. Thinking about a typical night in the last month, if you wake up, how long are you awake for in total? (add all the time up)
    1. 0-15 minutes (Add 4 points)
    2. 16-30 minutes (Add 3 points)
    3. 31-45 minutes (Add 2 points)
    4. 46-60 minutes (Add 1 points)
    5. 61 minutes or more

  4. Thinking about a typical night in the last month, how would you rate your sleep quality?
    1. Very good (Add 4 points)
    2. Good (Add 3 points)

    4. Poor (Add 1 points)
    5. Very poor
  5. Thinking about the past month, to what extent has poor sleep affected your mood, energy, or relationships?
    1. Not at all (Add 4 points)

    4. Much (Add 1 points)

  7. Thinking about the past month, to what extent has poor sleep troubled you in general?
    1. Not at all (Add 4 points)
    2. A little (Add 3 points)
    3. Somewhat (Add 2 points)
    4. Much (Add 1 points)
    5. Very much
  8. How long have you had a problem with your sleep?
    1. I don't have a problem (or I've had a problem for less than one month) (Add 4 points)

    3. 3-6 months (Add 2 points)
    4. 7-12 months (Add 1 points)
